Theseus (release )

Theseus is a unique tracker with the perfect combination of technlogies, giving you the best solution to locate your luggage.

Theseus is a GPS, GSM, LTE tracker; when GPS is poor, like in indoor positioning,Theseus overcomes the problem switching into the LTE technology.

The use Theseus is allowed in the airplane, because during the flight turns off, removing the possibilities of radio emissions.

You can know always its position ,just using the "update" botton.
